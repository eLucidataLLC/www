About

Coming from both an engineering and scientific background with shared experience in mapping, we at eLucidata help you clear the technical hurdles often encountered while investigating data. We work with our clients so they may develop the skills required to apply the data solutions we create together. For projects that call for more advanced analysis or visualizations, we can elevate the interpretability of your data far beyond the basics.

Doffy the Boss

Doffy is, we believe, a domestic shorthair feline best described as a Russian Blue version of Grumpy Cat. However, we have some reason to doubt this identification (not the grumpy part, that part we're pretty confident in) as "feline" and highly suspect that she may not be of this earth. Her primary mission on this planet is the protection and prompt daily emptying of her, and her feline-brother's, food bowls.

Bran Black

Bran has a geologic, oceanic, and environmental sciences background, the common thread being the application of remotely sensed imagery at hard-to-map sites. Beyond academic research, her mapping and teaching experience centers on helping scientists and students, particularly those unfamiliar with the mapping process, to collect and analyze their data.
